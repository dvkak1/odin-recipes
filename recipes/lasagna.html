Lasagna

Description

One of the oldest recipes of lasagna, it is a pasta that is known for its wide flat and sheet-like noodles. This is a recipe that is a must have in their rotation. Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling and a rich meaty tomato sauce make this a very palatable addition to restaurant menus everywhere.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 4 Cups of Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese
Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
Cheese mixture
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1 egg beaten

Cooking the Recipe

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. Cook pasta al dente according to package directions. Rinse under cold water and set aside.
  2. Cook beef, sausage, onion and garlic over medium high heat until no pink remains. Drain any fat.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning. Simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Make Cheese Mixture by combining 1 ½ cups mozzarella, ¼ cup parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, and egg.
  5. Add 1 cup meat sauce to a 9x13 pan. Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with 1/3 of the Cheese Mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce.
    Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce.
  6. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes.
  7. Uncover, sprinkle with remaining cheese (2 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup parmesan), and bake an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly.
    Broil 2-3 minutes if desired.
  8. Rest 10-15 minutes before cutting.
