Lasagna

Description

One of the oldest recipes of lasagna, it is a pasta that is known for its wide flat and sheet-like noodles. This is a recipe that is a must have in their rotation. Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling and a rich meaty tomato sauce make this a very palatable addition to restaurant menus everywhere.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 4 Cups of Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese
Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
Cheese mixture
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1 egg beaten
