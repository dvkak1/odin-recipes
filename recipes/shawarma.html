Chicken Shawarma

Description

One of the most widely known Levantine Arab dishes ever, Shawarma consists of a bread that is baked to be wrapped around meat cut into thin slices then roasted on a slowly turning vertical rotisserie or spit. This delicious dish is usually created with pieces of lamb, chicken, turkey, veal or beef.

Ingredients

  • 2lb / 1kg Chicken Thigh Fillets
Marinade
  • 1 Large Garlic Clove
  • 1 Tbsp Ground Coriander
  • 1 Tbsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 Tbsp Ground Cardamon
  • 1 Tbsp Ground Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
